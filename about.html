The People Behind Every Canvas

Two architects.
One standard.

Aditya TG
Founding Partner
"The guest should feel like the trip was made for them specifically. Because it was."

Aditya holds a PGDM in Retail & Ecommerce and began his hospitality journey in 2017 — organising curated overnight camps near Lonavala for travellers across India. He then spent years working with exceptional vacation rentals serving discerning clients across Mumbai and Pune, developing a sharp instinct for what truly luxury-minded guests expect before they know to ask for it. Pune-born, Aditya brought the canvas model — fixed pricing, zero itinerary reveals, total execution — to life when Nosh was founded in 2022.

Canvas Architecture Vacation Rentals SEO & Growth Community Builder
Akshay Manoj
Founding Partner
"A fixed price only means something if the experience it promises is actually delivered. Every time."

Akshay brings four years of Thailand and Vietnam ground expertise — two years in B2B DMC sales building trade relationships across Southeast Asia, followed by two years in direct B2C travel, understanding exactly what discerning travellers value and where standard operators fall short. Born in Thrissur, Kerala, he co-founded Nosh in 2022 to build the operations infrastructure that makes the fixed price promise genuinely unbreakable — the Nosh Brain system, the vendor network, the 24/7 WhatsApp protocol. 400+ groups delivered without a single checkout surprise is a system, not a coincidence.
