🔒 Nosh Flex Policy™

Change your blocks.
Before or after
the deposit.

The Nosh Flex Policy is our commitment: no block is locked permanently. Even after you've paid the 30% confirmation deposit, any block can be swapped, upgraded or removed — no penalties from Nosh Tours' end.

Before payment — Change any block freely. We re-quote instantly at no charge.

After 30% deposit — Still flexible. Block swaps re-priced transparently. Any price difference adjusted in the balance.

No Nosh penalty fees — We never charge a change fee. Third-party cancellation policies (airlines, hotels) may apply and will be disclosed upfront.

30% confirms your booking — Balance due before departure. No full upfront payment required.
